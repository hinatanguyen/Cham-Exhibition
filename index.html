Chấm isn't just a full stop…
It's pressure, clash, flavor,
and the spark of something new.

Much like the punctuation itself, Chấm marks the end of an unforgettable journey, one where we all have been challenged and tested, our skills honed and perfected. It also sets forth the start of our own separate paths, to the far reaches of the world, to the unknown. The end, yet the beginning.

Chấm Exhibition is an exhibition showcasing the works of Capstone 2 students from Hanoi. 13 works from 13 students showcasing their knowledge and skills through a variety of design disciplines, including: Branding, Marketing, Illustration, Graphic Design, Publication, UI/UX, and Spatial Design.
